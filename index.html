Our Story

Crafting Elegance Since 1985

Founded in a small workshop in Florence, VS Shoes has been dedicated to creating the finest leather footwear for over three decades. Our journey began with a simple philosophy: to combine traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design.

Today, each pair of VS Shoes is still handcrafted by our master artisans who bring their decades of experience to every stitch. We source only the finest leathers from reputable tanneries in Italy and England, ensuring quality that stands the test of time.
